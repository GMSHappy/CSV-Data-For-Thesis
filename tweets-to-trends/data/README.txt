DATA FOLDER — From Tweets to Trends
=====================================
Girish | TU Dublin | BSc Computing 2025

Place these three files in this folder:

  1. CleanTrump2.csv
     Your cleaned Trump tweets from KNIME
     Required columns: id, created_at, text, retweets, favorites, author

  2. CleanElon.csv
     Your cleaned Elon Musk tweets from KNIME
     Required columns: id, created_at, text, retweets, favorites, author

  3. World_Events_200.csv
     Convert your World_Events_200.xlsx to CSV first
     Required columns: date, year, event_description, category, link

NOTE: The dashboard will still run without the CSV files
using built-in sample data, but your real data will give
accurate results for your thesis presentation.

DATE FORMAT
-----------
created_at should be:  2023-05-07 10:35:22
category values:       Political, Economic, Technology, Social
